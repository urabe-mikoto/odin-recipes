Curry Rice

Description

For when you need some comfort in life!

Ingredients

  • 400 g ground beef as your choice!!!
  • 1 onions, minced medium
  • 2 carrots, small chunks
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 800 ml water
  • 200 boiled chickpeas, lightly mash use more if you don't use meat
  • 4 tomatoes, boiled and peeled
  • 190 g homemade curry roux

Steps

To Prepare the Ingredients

  1. Chop the onion into small pieces and cut the carrots into small chunks.
  2. Lightly mash the boiled chickpeas.

To Make Boiled and Peeled Tomatoes

  1. Remove the stem of the tomato and make a cross-shaped shallow slit on the opposite side of the stem.
  2. Put the tomatoes into boiled water and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Place in ice water and peel off the tomato skin with your fingers.

To Cook the Curry

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat and saut√© the onions until they become translucent and tender.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until it's no longer pink.
  3. Add the carrot and mix well to coat with oil.
  4. Add a water and bay leaf and bring it to a boil. Once boiling, using a small fine-mesh strainer, skim the scrum from the surface of the broth.
  5. Cover and simmer over medium heat for 20 minutes. If the heat is too high, lower the heat slightly.
  6. (You can make curry roux from scratch while the curry ingredients are simmering.)
  7. When the carrots are tender, add the chickpeas and cook for 2-3 minutes.

To Add the Curry Roux

  1. Add the pre-cooked curry roux powder to the pot.
  2. Stir slowly over low heat until melted.
  3. Cook over low heat for 5 minutes until the sauce begins to thicken. Be careful not to get burned and stuck to the pan.
